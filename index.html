Hi. I'm Clarissa.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling and the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in books and YouTube videos, I can be found singing, contemplating life, and taking photos of anything and everything.

I was born and raised in Southern California, and it's the place I call my home. Though I love my own little corner of the world, I enjoy traveling beyond its borders - experiencing new places through new faces and good food with good company.

My passion: I love to make music, and I believe that through song, we are able to express emotion and share experiences in ways that words can't do alone. There's nothing I love more than lyrics and melodies that bring life to any given moment.

A fun fact: As much as I find myself getting lost in the world of calligraphy through Instagram, I also find just as much joy creating pieces of my own. You'll often find me lettering during work breaks and dipping nibs into dinky dips of sumi ink.
